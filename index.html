MIKEY'S GYROS

We started Mikey's in Moscow, ID in 1995. Since then our priority has always been to provide our customers with high quality, fresh and healthy food, served in a fast and friendly manner – offered at a competitive price point. Our menu selections reflect the authentic Mediterranean food with a relaxed atmosphere which has made us favorite with the local residents. We hope you’ll join us for lunch or dinner and become part of our family.
